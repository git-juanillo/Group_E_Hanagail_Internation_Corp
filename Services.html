Maintenance is Essential

PVC curtains are durable, but regular care is necessary to ensure they continue to perform their primary functions.

  • Hardware Check & Lubrication:We inspect and lubricate the mounting rails and roller systems.
  • Strip Inspection & Cleaning: We check for tears, chips, and excessive scratching.
  • Alignment & Tension Adjustment: We realign the strips to restore the critical overlap, ensuring the thermal seal remains intact and effective.

A maintenance plan extends the curtain lifespan by up to 50%, minimizes energy loss, and keeps your facility safe and compliant year-round.

Repairs Matter

A damaged PVC strip curtain instantly loses its effectiveness as a barrier. That is why we offer;

  • Strip ReplacementWe replace individual damaged, torn, or discolored strips only—not the entire curtain.
  • Hardware Repair: Repair or replacement of broken mounting plates, hanger rails, or retaining bars
  • Strip Re-Alignment: Correcting misalignment and adjusting the overlap caused by vehicle impact or heavy use.
  • Emergency Response: Fast scheduling for critical damage affecting safety or production environments.

About Us

Building trust through reliable products and dedicated service since 2015.

Company Overview

HIC is a company providing a wide range of PVC Strip Curtains Supply and Services including repairs, installation, and preventive maintenance. Our team is composed of highly trained personnel dedicated to delivering top-notch service and exceeding customer expectations.


We ensure fast, reliable, and high-quality services that help clients maintain a pleasant working environment and smooth business operations.


Around six years ago, HIC expanded its services to meet the growing needs of customers. This expansion included Epoxy Injection Supply & Services, Mechanical Works, Fabrication, Rackings System Installations, Packaging Materials, and even air-conditioning repair and cleaning.
